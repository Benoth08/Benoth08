## Featured Project: EXALIA (GenAI)

### **Hybrid Liquid Neural Architecture**
*Status: Advanced Prototype*

Development of an experimental architecture combining Large Language Models (LLMs) with continuous-time neural networks (Liquid Networks) for advanced causal reasoning.

**Technical Stack:** `PyTorch` `Hugging Face (Qwen)` `Neural Circuit Policies` `torchdiffeq`

**Engineering Approach:**
- **Architecture:** Integration of Closed-form Continuous-time (CfC) networks within frozen Transformer backbones.
- **Mechanism:** Sparse routing directing static knowledge to pre-trained layers and dynamic states to liquid neural networks.
- **Innovation:** Solves the "static logic" limitation of LLMs by introducing temporal dynamics for multi-step reasoning.
